How to sign up on Codedex?

  1. Open a web browser and go to codedex.io.
  2. Click Sign Up in the top-right corner.
  3. Create an account using an email address or sign in with Google/GitHub.
  4. Verify your email if prompted.
  5. Log in to your Codedex account.
  6. From the dashboard, choose a programming language (e.g., Python).
  7. Click Start Course.
  8. Write code directly in the provided editor.
  9. Click Run or Submit to check your solution.
  10. Fix errors if shown, then resubmit.
  11. Continue to the next lesson after passing.
